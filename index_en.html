Greeting

ELLIPSYS is an IT solutions company founded in 2021. The name “ELLIPSYS” is a coined term combining “ELLIPSIS,” meaning omission, and “SYS,” representing systems. It embodies our vision: to create a society where technology removes waste and people can focus on creativity.

Guided by this philosophy, we provide integrated services across planning, design, and development, aiming to deliver truly valuable experiences to users.

We believe technology is not just for convenience, but for solving real problems and making people’s daily lives better.

We always put ourselves in the users' and clients' shoes, carefully identifying what truly matters, and facing technology sincerely.

Freer, smarter experiences— that's the future we shape, every single day.
